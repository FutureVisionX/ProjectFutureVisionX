About

FutureVisionX is a student-led innovation club on a mission to explore, invent, and inspire through hands-on STEM projects, challenges, and ideas. I first started this initiative alone and I hope to build an engaging community through this club. Through fun weekly challenges, workshops, and outreach, this organization empowers students to problem-solve, explore, invent, and lead with a purpose.

This isn't a club, it's a movement — where future leaders, inventors, and changemakers come together to shape the world into a better place.
